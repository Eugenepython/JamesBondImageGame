Welcome to the James Bond image guessing game!

Click 'start' to answer a series of 15 questions. On each question, you will have the option of matching the image to the James Bond movie by clicking the correct Bond movie button. If you click without unbluring the image at all, you will get 3 points. If you unblur again, and are correct, you will get 2 points. If you unblur again until it is fully clear you will get 1 point if correct. If incorrect at all, you will get 0 points. The maximum score will be 45. Good luck!
